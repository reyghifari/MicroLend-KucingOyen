✨ Features

Why Choose MicroLend?

A secure, transparent, and fair micro-lending ecosystem built on blockchain technology.

🔒

Stablecoin Collateral

Every loan is secured with stablecoin collateral, providing protection for lenders and peace of mind for all participants.

⭐

Reputation Tiering

A 5-level system from Starter to Elite that rewards consistent borrowers with lower collateral requirements as their reputation grows.

💰

Fair Interest System

Borrowers with good reputations receive benefits without reducing the yield that lenders receive. Fair for both sides.

🔐

Privacy by Design

Built on Canton's privacy-preserving architecture — only the borrower and lender can view loan details.

📊

Full Audit Trail

Complete action history for every loan, enabling compliance reporting and transparent record-keeping.

⚡

Automated Workflows

The entire loan lifecycle — from request to repayment — is automated through Daml smart contracts.

How It Works

Simple, transparent, and automated from start to finish.

1

Connect

Users connect to the platform with their unique Party ID on Canton Network.

2

Create Request

Borrowers submit loan requests specifying amount, interest rate, due date, and collateral.

3

Review & Approve

Lenders review requests with collateral info and borrower tier, then approve or decline.

4

Repay & Complete

Borrowers repay through the interface. Collateral releases and reputation score updates.
